asnMobileRegisterDevice

(Re-)register a new line to the virtual line subsystem of the server.

For testscenarios or other usecases it is possible to resgster a line on the server, which than appears as a normal phone line.
The client is than responsible for the behaviour of this line and can emulate/act as a phone line.
